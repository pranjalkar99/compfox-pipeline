applied a personal jurisdiction analysis to conclude that the WCAB has no jurisdiction over applicants claim. However, there is no question that the WCAB has personal jurisdiction over applicant and defendants with regard to the claim of industrial injury.The WCAB has personal jurisdiction over applicant because he acceded to WCAB jurisdiction over his person when he filed his claim with

the WCAB. The WCAB also has personal jurisdiction over the defendants because the Bills generally appeared to contest applicants claim. The Bills have sufficient contact with California to be subject to WCAB personal jurisdiction because the team purposefully availed itself of the privilege of conducting business in California by taking deliberate action to play games in the state and by creating a continuing obligation to the residents of the state by scheduling future games in California.
